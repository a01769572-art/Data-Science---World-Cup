T-01-03 - Identity poisoning: fuzzy or incomplete mappings can silently merge distinct teams or drop participants. Mitigate with source-keyed reviewed aliases, foreign-key checks, ambiguity rejection, and hard coverage reports.

T-01-04 - Score semantic corruption: shootout advancement may be confused with the score, or extra-time scores may be mislabeled as 90-minute results. Mitigate by preserving martj42 scores unchanged, storing a separate nullable shootout winner, and documenting the exact source semantics in schema metadata and tests.
</threat_model>
